  • RecipeGo

The project's goal is to create a vibrant, interactive space for food lovers to connect, share their experiences, and learn from one another.

  • Pano

    The aim of this project is to create a photography website where both new and experienced photographers can share the pictures they have taken, connect with other photographers, and build a community around their shared passion. The website will be designed to showcase the beauty and artistry of photography, and provide users with an interactive platform to showcase their work. With an emphasis on community building, the project's goal is to create a vibrant and supportive space for photographers of all levels to share their art, improve their skills, and form lasting connections with others who share their passion.

    The photography website will feature a user-friendly interface that will allow users to create their own profiles and upload their pictures. Users will be able to share their work, receive feedback, and engage with other photographers through comments and direct messages. The website will also offer a browsing function that will allow users to search for pictures by category, such as landscape, portrait, or street photography, and by location or other tags.

  • film roulette

    The concept of this web development project revolves around indie films. The website offers a unique experience for film enthusiasts by streaming a new indie film every week for subscribers. The film is shown live for everyone at the same time, and subscribers can chat with each other during the film, making it a social experience that is akin to Twitch.tv for films.

    The indie film website is designed to showcase independent films and help aspiring filmmakers gain exposure. The website provides a platform for filmmakers to submit their independent films, which are then reviewed by a team of curators who select the films that will be featured on the website. With a focus on community engagement, the website also features a chat function that allows subscribers to engage in discussions and debates with other film enthusiasts, creating an interactive and immersive experience. The project's goal is to provide a space where indie films can be celebrated and shared, and to foster a community of film lovers who appreciate the creativity and innovation of independent filmmakers.
